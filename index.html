About Me

I'm a Robotics Software Engineer with a Master's in Robotics Engineering from WPI. I currently work at ChemGenes Corporation, where I build automation systems for lab processes — integrating hardware and writing the software that drives it.

I enjoy working close to the metal with C++, ROS2, and embedded systems to build reliable, efficient real-time systems. My interests lie in robotic manipulation, legged robots, optimal control, and reinforcement learning. I love to see my code come to life on a robot and wouldn’t mind getting my hands dirty to fix any hardware.

Lately, I've been focused on improving my software development skills and experimenting with RL-based manipulation using the LeRobot SO-101 arm — exploring the world of “Physical AI.”
